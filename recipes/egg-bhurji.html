Complete Cooking Instructions

Follow these step-by-step instructions to create the perfect Egg Bhurji

1

Prepare the Eggs

Lightly whisk the eggs in a bowl with a pinch of salt. Don't over-beat; you want streaks of yolk and white visible.

üí° Room temperature eggs scramble more evenly than cold ones.

üí° Tomatoes should break down completely to form a rich base.

üí° Blooming spices releases their full aroma and flavor.
6
